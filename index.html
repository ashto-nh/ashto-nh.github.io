Boomerang Platformer Prototype


My current project is the Boomerang Platformer Prototype! It's a work in progress game about chaining together movements with your boomerang-oriented abilities. The player can jump off of a boomerang, can teleport with a boomerang, can use switches with a boomerang, and so much more!

Right now, my plans are:

  1. ⚙ Port to Godot 4.0
  2. 🔧 Fix HTML5 port
  3. 🪃 Finish remaining platforming systems
  4. ✨ POLISH THE HECK OUT OF THIS THING!!!
